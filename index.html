Hello!

I'm Nelly, I'm 25 and from Stroud, Gloucestershire.

Having trained at Newcastle University for 4 years I qualified in 2018 as a Speech and Language Therapist. I have worked for the NHS for around 5 years in various different fields (children's therapy services, public health, administration).

I decided to apply for the Mayden Academy to train as a full stack software developer as I could see from my previous experience in health care many areas that could be facilitated by technical solutions. I also have some personal projects I would like to put into fruition but I previously didn't have the technical skills to do so!

I now work as a software developer at a health tech company, Mayden, working on software for mental health services to help improve patients' access to psychological therapies.

Alongside my work, I have a keen interest in travel and speaking modern foreign languages; cooking (and eating!); and yoga.
