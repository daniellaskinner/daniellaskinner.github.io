About me

Hello! I'm Nelly, I'm 24 and from Stroud, Gloucestershire.

Having trained at Newcastle University for 4 years I qualified in 2018 as a Speech and Language Therapist. I have worked for the NHS for 5 years in various different fields (children's therapy services, public health, administration).

I decided to apply for the Mayden Academy to train as a full stack software developer as I could see from my previous experience in health care many areas that could be facilitated by technical solutions. I also have some personal projects I would like to put into fruition but I previously didn't have the technical skills to do so!

Alongside my work, I have a keen interest in travel and speaking modern foreign languages, cooking (and eating!), and yoga. I am currently based in Bath, Somerset.
